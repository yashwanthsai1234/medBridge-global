Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our platform.

Is MedBridge Global a pharmacy or medication seller? +

No, MedBridge Global is not a pharmacy or medication seller. We are a technology platform that facilitates connections between verified hospital suppliers in India and consumers in the US. We do not sell, store, or handle any medications or medical equipment directly.

How do you ensure product quality and safety? +

All suppliers on our platform are verified hospitals or authorized medical distributors in India. These suppliers must meet our strict verification requirements and provide certification for their products. We regularly audit our supplier network to maintain quality standards.

How much can I save compared to US prices? +

Savings vary by product, but many customers save 50-80% compared to US prices, even when factoring in shipping costs. In many cases, the cost is less than typical insurance copays for the same medications or equipment.

How long does shipping take? +

Shipping times typically range from 7-14 business days, depending on the supplier and destination. Expedited shipping options are available from some suppliers for an additional fee.

Is this legal? +

Yes. We focus on non-patented medications, non-prescription medicines, medical equipment, and health supplements that can be legally imported for personal use. Our platform simply facilitates connections between verified suppliers and consumers, similar to other marketplace models. We recommend consulting with a healthcare professional regarding any medications.
