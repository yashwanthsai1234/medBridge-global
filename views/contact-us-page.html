Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

How can I become a registered user? +

You can register on our platform by clicking the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page. Fill in the required information and verify your email address. Once registered, you'll have access to browse products, compare prices, and place orders.

How do I contact a supplier directly? +

Once you've created an account and verified your credentials, you can browse our verified suppliers and contact them directly through our platform's messaging system. This ensures all communications are documented and secure.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We support various payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and digital payment options. All payments are processed securely through our platform to protect both buyers and suppliers.

How are shipping and delivery handled? +

Shipping is handled directly by our verified suppliers. Delivery times vary depending on the product and destination. All shipments include tracking information and meet international shipping regulations for healthcare products.

Can I become a supplier on your platform? +

We welcome new suppliers who meet our strict verification standards. Please contact our supplier relations team at suppliers@medbridge.global to begin the verification process and learn more about joining our network.
